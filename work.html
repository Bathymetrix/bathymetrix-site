Current engagement

Bathymetrix is currently under joint contract with Princeton University and EarthScope-Oceans
to manage the Mobile Earthquake Recording in Marine Areas by Independent Divers (MERMAID) array.

Contract deliverables

Software and data management

Bathymetrix manages the full lifecycle of data collected by MERMAID floats, from wrangling through curation and archival at the EarthScope Data Management Center, making MERMAID data available to scientists worldwide. We emphasize clean, maintainable code and develop open workflows for long-term use through GitHub.

Original research and publications

Bathymetrix leads original research projects and consults with scientists interested in utilizing MERMAID data. We built the software pipeline used to associate untagged acoustic data with natural events and pick seismoacoustic arrival times. Recently, we led the development of a whole-ocean-basin acoustic attenuation model.
